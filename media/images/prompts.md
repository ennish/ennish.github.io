# 生存斩杀线 (The Execute Line) - AI 绘图提示词指南

为了增强互动故事的沉浸感，以下为每个核心章节设计的 AI 绘图提示词。建议使用 Midjourney (v6) 或 Stable Diffusion (XL) 运行。

## 核心视觉风格 (General Style)
**Style**: Cinematic, hyper-realistic, gritty, dim lighting, rainy atmosphere, cold color grading (blue, gray, charcoal), high contrast.
**Theme**: Cyber-noir realism (no flying cars, just cold industrial dystopia).

---

## 1. 启程 (The Arrival)
*场景：西雅图塔科马机场出口，细雨，冷色调。*
> **Prompt**: Cinematic shot of a lone immigrant standing outside Seattle-Tacoma international airport, raining, gray sky, holding a worn-out suitcase, blurred airport lights in background, moody lighting, hyper-realistic, 8k, cold color palette.

## 2. 港口码头 (Seattle Harbor)
*场景：巨大的集装箱，锈迹斑斑，咸腥的海风。*
> **Prompt**: Massive rusty shipping containers stacked high at Seattle harbor, seaside morning mist, cold industrial seaport, cranes in silhouette, wet asphalt, gritty texture, realistic cinematic lighting.

## 3. 家禽加工厂 (The Poultry Plant)
*场景：刺眼的日光灯，冷库的白雾，血腥与工业的结合。*
> **Prompt**: Interior of a cold poultry processing factory, sterilized white light, hanging chicken carcasses on a moving rail, workers in blue plastic aprons and hairnets, steam and fog, industrial cold storage atmosphere, photorealistic.

## 4. 斩杀线下的危机 (Below the Execute Line)
*场景：窄小的廉价出租屋或车内。*
> **Prompt**: First-person view of a cramped, messy basement room in Seattle, a pile of unpaid bills and a single $1 dollar bill on a rickety table, flickering warm light bulb, damp walls, sense of poverty and despair, 8k resolution.

## 5. ICE 袭扰 (The Raid)
*场景：蓝红交替的警灯，惊恐的阴影。*
> **Prompt**: Flashing red and blue police lights reflecting on a wet factory floor, dramatic shadows of SWAT officers in silhouette, panic, blurred motion, cinematic suspense, high contrast noir style.

## 6. 流离失所 (Displacement)
*场景：破旧二手车里的“家”。*
> **Prompt**: Interior of an old beat-up car at night, rain streaking on windows, a thin blanket on the back seat, Seattle city skyline lights blurred in the distance, loneliness, cold atmosphere, highly detailed.

## 7. 结局：鱼饵 (Ending: The Bait)
*场景：桥洞下的寂静。*
> **Prompt**: Underneath a dark Seattle bridge at dawn, gray mist over the water, a heap of discarded clothes and trash by the riverbank, melancholic, haunting beauty, cinematic wide shot, pale morning light.

## 8. 结局：维权者 (Ending: The Resister)
*场景：地下室里的传单，微弱但坚定的光。*
> **Prompt**: A close-up of a hand holding a labor union flyer "United Workers", dimly lit community basement, warm candlelight, old typewriter, sense of hope and rebellion, gritty but warm lighting.
